# Contributing to the One-Click Blueprint

We love contributions! By sharing your agent templates, you help the entire community build a more useful AI workforce.

## How to Contribute a Template

1.  **Fork this repository.**
2.  **Create your template:** Add your `.md` or `.json` configuration to the `community_templates/` folder.
3.  **Use a clear name:** e.g., `TravelPlanner_Agent.md` or `LegalResearch_Prompt.json`.
4.  **Submit a Pull Request:** Explain what your agent does and how it helps.

## Contribution Guidelines
- **No Private Data:** Ensure your templates don't contain real names, keys, or passwords.
- **Explain the "Why":** Briefly describe the problem your agent solves.
- **Tested & Proven:** Only submit templates you've actually used and verified.
