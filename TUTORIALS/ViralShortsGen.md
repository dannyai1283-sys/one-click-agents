# Tutorial: Viral Shorts Gen

## Overview
Generate high-retention short-form video scripts for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

## Usage
1. Select a hook from `hooks.json`.
2. Fill in your content in `script_template.md`.
3. The agent will optimize the wording for brevity and engagement.

## Tips
- Keep your sentences short.
- Use the 'Problem-Agitate-Solve' framework provided in the template.
