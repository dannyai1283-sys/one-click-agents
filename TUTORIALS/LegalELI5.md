# Tutorial: Legal ELI5

## Overview
Break down complex legal jargon into simple, understandable English.

## Usage
1. Paste the legal text into a new Markdown file.
2. Run the Legal ELI5 agent.
3. Review the simplified summary focusing on the areas defined in `summarizer_config.json`.

## Warning
This is for informational purposes only. Consult a lawyer for actual legal advice.
